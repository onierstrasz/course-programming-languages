Welcome to Snakes and Ladders: Jack starts!

Jack and Jill take turns rolling a die and moving forward. If they land on the bottom of a ladder, they go up; if they land on the head of a snake, they slither down. If they land on an occupied square, they must go home. If they go past the end, they turn and go backward. The first to land exactly on the last square wins.
